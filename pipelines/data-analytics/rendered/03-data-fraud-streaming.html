Fraud Decision Routing (Score-Based)
High Confidence (score > 0.9) — Auto-Block
1. Transaction blocked immediately (card-present: <100ms)
2. SNS notification → PagerDuty for fraud ops team
3. Customer SMS: "Unusual activity detected"
4. Card frozen pending verification
Volume: ~0.08% of transactions (36/hour at peak)
Medium Confidence (0.5–0.9) — Manual Review
1. Transaction allowed (avoids false-positive friction)
2. Queued to SQS → fraud analyst review queue
3. Analyst has 4-hour SLA to review + decide
4. If confirmed fraud: retroactive block + chargeback
Volume: ~0.24% of transactions (108/hour at peak)
