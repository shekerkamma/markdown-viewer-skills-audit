Group: Decision Factors (Enterprise Evaluation Criteria)
Cost
$0.01–$0.50/invocation depending on model. Managed agents: $0.08/session-hour. CAC for 1000-agent deployment: $5K-$50K/mo.
Reliability
Hallucination rate: 2-5% for constrained tasks, 69-88% for complex legal queries. Multi-agent systems amplify errors.
Security
40% of enterprises cite security as primary scaling obstacle. Risks: prompt injection, data leakage, tool access sprawl.
Integration Ease
Claude SDK: simplest (1 API). LangChain: most connectors (200+). Enterprise SSO, audit trail, RBAC requirements.
Community & Support
LangChain: largest community. CrewAI: fastest-growing Discord. Claude SDK: Anthropic enterprise support tier.
